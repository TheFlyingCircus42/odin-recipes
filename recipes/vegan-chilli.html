Vegan Chilli



A delicious and warming mushroom and vegan chilli that is packed full of goodness! This recipe has been a staple in my kitchen for a long time because it is both tasty and perfect for batch cooking. The recipe calls for three different beans, but you can easily swap them out for your favourites.

Ingredients

  • 1x White Onion (chopped)
  • 3-5 Cloves of Garlic (minced)
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of tomato pure
  • 2x Aubergines (optional)
  • 2x 400g Tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 500g Chopped mushrooms
  • 1-2 Medium carrots (diced into 1cm cubes)
  • 2-3 Sticks of celery (diced into 1cm cubes)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Chilli Powder
  • Maple Syrup
  • 1x Stock cube or gel
