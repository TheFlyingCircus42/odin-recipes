Honey Lemon And Ginger Tea

If you looking for a caffiene free alternative to your regular hot drink then this quick honey, lemon and ginger tea is sure to hit the spot. A soothing drink that is great at any time of day.

Ingredient

  • Hot water
  • Lemon
  • Ginger
  • Honey

Recipe

  1. Slice a piece of lemon, and then slice again into two halves and add them to the cup.
  2. Slice 3-4 pieces of ginger (long, thin slices work best) and add to the cup.
  3. Take 1 generous teaspoon of honey and hold it in the cup.
  4. Pour the hot water directly on to the honey on the spoon, and stir well.
  5. Relax and enjoy!
